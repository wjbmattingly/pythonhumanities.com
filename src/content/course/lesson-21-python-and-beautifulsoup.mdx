When working with web data, `requests` and BeautifulSoup are typically used together. Requests is good at fetching the page; it's not very good at parsing the result or pulling specific pieces out. BeautifulSoup handles both of those.

This lesson shows how to pass the response from `requests` into BeautifulSoup and start navigating the resulting parse tree — finding tags, classes, and the data you actually want.
